Comprehensive Legal Services

Expert litigation and property law services with strategic focus across England & Wales

Residential Possession Proceedings

Procedural Precision. Strategic Litigation.

Residential possession proceedings are highly technical. Errors in notice drafting, deposit compliance, licensing status or evidential preparation frequently determine outcome.

We advise landlords and occupiers from initial notice stage through to contested hearing and enforcement.

Pre-Proceedings Strategy

Before issuing proceedings, we assess tenancy classification, statutory notice requirements, deposit compliance, prescribed information history, licensing exposure, and risk of disrepair counterclaim.

Early analysis reduces adjournments and cost exposure.

Section 8 Proceedings

We advise on ground selection, mandatory vs discretionary grounds, arrears evidence, breach of tenancy, anti-social behaviour claims, and drafting pleadings capable of withstanding defence.

Each defence is assessed on evidential merit and legal viability.

Section 21 (Where Applicable)

We assess validity and service compliance, prescribed documentation, retaliatory eviction exposure, and evidential integrity.

Misclassification can invalidate proceedings. Careful legal analysis is essential.

Notices to Quit & Non-Assured Occupation

We advise on common law tenancies, licence termination, lodgers and excluded occupiers, tenancy at will, and occupation status disputes.

Rent-to-Rent & Layered Occupation

Multi-tier occupation arrangements require structured litigation planning.

We advise on head landlord rights, intermediate operator liability, sub-tenant status, multi-defendant proceedings, and enforcement logistics.

Defending Possession Claims

We represent occupiers defending possession proceedings, including disrepair counterclaims, tenancy deposit disputes, procedural invalidity challenges, discretionary mitigation, and Equality Act considerations.

Each defence is assessed on evidential merit and legal viability.

Illegal Eviction & Urgent Applications

We advise and represent in lock changes, harassment allegations, injunction applications, emergency hearings, and damages strategy.

Enforcement

Following possession orders including warrants, suspension applications, strategic enforcement planning.

Enforcement should be practical, not performative.

Commercial Possession & Forfeiture

Structured advice. Commercial litigation.

Commercial lease disputes require decisive and technically precise action.

We advise on Section 146 notices, waiver risk, breach strategy, arrears forfeiture, relief from forfeiture applications, contested County Court hearings, and enforcement strategy.

Commercial litigation demands structure and clarity.

Lease Extensions & Disputes

Statutory compliance. Strategic planning.

We advise leaseholders and freeholders in statutory lease extensions, counter-notice strategy, negotiated extensions, premium disputes, service charge litigation, and tribunal preparation.

These matters require procedural discipline and evidential precision.

Neighbourhood & Boundary Disputes

Evidence-driven litigation. Strategic resolution.

We advise in boundary disputes, title interpretation, surveyor coordination, trespass claims, rights of way disputes, easement claims, injunction applications, and County Court proceedings.

Evidence-driven litigation prevents escalation.

Debt Recovery & Enforcement

Commercial recovery strategy. Practical solutions.

We provide structured recovery strategy including pre-action correspondence, letter before claim, settlement negotiation, money claims, defended proceedings, judgment enforcement, charging orders, attachment of earnings, and third party debt orders.

Strategic recovery planning with practical enforcement.

Nationwide Court Representation

Solicitor-led advocacy. Measured representation.

We represent clients in County Court hearings across England & Wales in possession hearings, commercial possession, relief from forfeiture, suspension of warrants, interim injunctions, money claim trials, and enforcement proceedings.

Effective advocacy is built before the hearing. We review pleadings, assess compliance and prepare structured submissions.
