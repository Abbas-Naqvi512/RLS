Commercial Property Forfeiture

Successfully represented commercial landlord in high-value forfeiture proceedings following tenant breach of lease covenants.

Challenge: Tenant operating unauthorized restaurant business from retail premises, breaching multiple lease conditions.

Strategy: Comprehensive breach analysis, Section 146 notice preparation, waiver risk assessment, and immediate County Court application.

Outcome: Successful forfeiture order obtained with immediate enforcement rights

Value: £850,000 annual rent secured

Complex Residential Possession

Defended residential possession claim involving multiple procedural defenses and counterclaims.

Challenge: Landlord serving invalid Section 21 notice with deposit protection non-compliance and licensing violations.

Strategy: Technical defense preparation, deposit counterclaim, procedural invalidity arguments, and settlement negotiation.

Outcome: Claim discontinued against landlord with favorable settlement terms

Result: Tenant secured property with reduced rent and landlord paid full legal costs

Boundary & Easement Dispute

Resolved complex boundary dispute between commercial properties affecting development rights.

Challenge: Historical boundary ambiguity affecting multi-million pound development project with conflicting surveyor reports.

Outcome: Boundary agreement reached enabling development to proceed
